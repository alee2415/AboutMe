My Mobile Closet

Designed as a comprehensive solution for organizing and enhancing wardrobe management, my portfolio project is a mobile closet app. Users can effortlessly upload images of their clothing items, enabling the app to suggest outfit combinations based on color, mood, or brand preferences. Additionally, the app streamlines item addition by allowing users to scan barcodes from clothing stores directly. With integrated chat functionality, users can discuss styling options or seek fashion advice. In future iterations, the app will transition to virtual reality, offering an immersive experience where friends can interact within their personalized virtual closets.
